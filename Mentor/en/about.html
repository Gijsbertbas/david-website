About me

 

After more than 10 years in the field of geology, I started accompanying individuals through challenging life experiences, following hence another deeply-rooted aspiration. My expertise relies on:

a formation in maieusthesia completed between 2018 and 2021 (certified practitioner)

a delicate sensitivity and a profound wonderment for what we are

a life path made of diversified experiences

My name is David Bréthaut and I was born in 1981. I grew up in Geneva, Switzerland. Passionate as I was about the history of life and our planet, I completed a master’s degree in geology. For about fifteen years, my studies and subsequent career in the geothermal and hydrocarbon industries took me from Switzerland to Spain, the United States, New Zealand and the Netherlands. I moved back to Geneva recently with my wife and our two children.

Driven since my early childhood by a strong need for authenticity, life brought me to look into myself with a special attention and deeply question what and who I was. Helping others in their existence started progressively to become a clear aspiration over the last few years. I have now put aside my career in the energy sector. Occasionally, I organize geological excursions and hikes. My counselling activities become more and more present in my day-to-day life. It makes me happy.
