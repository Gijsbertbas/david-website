About me

a few words about myself and what inspires me

 

My name is David Bréthaut and I was born in 1981. I grew up in Geneva, Switzerland. Passionate as I was about the history of life and our planet, I completed a master’s degree in geology. For about fifteen years, my studies and subsequent career in the geothermal and hydrocarbon industries took me from Switzerland to Spain, the United States, New Zealand and the Netherlands. I am now living in The Hague with my wife and our two children.

Despite a childhood without seeming issues, I always had to deal with anxiety. As I grew up, it came to be problematic in my day to day life. In my early thirties, some changes brought me to deeply question what and who I was. I started to see certain aspects of my past under a new light. Little by little I discovered more about myself and it strongly impacted my life. Now everything feels wider and more authentic. This new adventure is not free from suffering, yet it is rich and full of touching discoveries. Our inner world is for me a constant source of wonder.

Helping others in their existence started progressively to become a clear aspiration. Between 2018 and 2020 I took a course in maieusthesia. It is an approach of a profound humanity. It considers life in a way that corresponds to my sensitivity and intuition and I am very grateful to have encountered it.

I have now completed all modules of the course and have started to practice within the scope of taking the certification exam. I have put aside my career in the energy sector. Occasionally, I organize geological excursions and hikes. And I am happy to see that my activities in psychotherapy become more and more present in my day to day life.
