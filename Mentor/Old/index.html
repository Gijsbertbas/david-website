Welcome

I offer personal counselling sessions based on a respectful and benevolent approach called maieusthesia.

My practice is addressed to people experiencing a wide range of symptoms, such as:

  • stress or anxiety
  • sleep or eeting disorders
  • burnout
  • depression
  • family or relationship problems

I work as well with people willing to get to know themselves better or grow in an area of their existence in which they feel limited.

The psychotherapeutic approach that I offer is called maieusthesia. In this approach, psychological and psychosomatic difficulties are regarded as precious expressions pointing towards parts of ourselves that are calling for attention. During therapy sessions, it is the greeting and the acknowledgement of those parts of ourselves, in the full dimension of what they are and how they feel, that permits to find an inner integrity that was hitherto pending. Hence if the symptoms disappear, it is not because they have been “cured”, but because they just have no reason to persist. In maieusthesia, particular consideration is given to feelings and sensations. This work is done in a respectful, open and benevolent atmosphere.
