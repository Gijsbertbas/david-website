About me

A brief summary.

David Bréthaut

My name is David Bréthaut. I was born and raised in Geneva, Switzerland. Passionate as I was about the history of life and our planet, I studied geology. For about fifteen years, my studies and subsequent career in the geothermal and hydrocarbon industries took me from Switzerland to Spain, the United States, New Zealand and the Netherlands. I am now living in The Hague with my wife and our two children.

Despite a childhood without seeming issues, I always had to deal with anxiety. As I grew up, it came to be problematic in my day to day life. In my early thirties, I became conscious of certain aspects of my past that I had never realized before. This change strongly impacted my life and brought me to deeply question what and who I was. This was the start of a new adventure, which is not free from suffering, yet rich and full of touching discoveries. Our inner world is fascinating, and has become for me a constant source of wonder.

I have now put aside my career in the energy sector and occasionally organize geological excursions and hikes. Meanwhile, in 2018 I started a training in maieusthesia. I am now reaching the end of this course and have started to practice within the scope of taking the certification exam. I love what I do, and I strongly believe in this therapeutic approach.
